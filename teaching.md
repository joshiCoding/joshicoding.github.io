# Teaching

I am passionate about teaching Computer Science and believe that students learn best through a combination of strong theoretical foundations, practical programming experience, and research-oriented thinking. My goal is to make complex topics accessible through visual demonstrations, hands-on programming, and real-world applications.

# Teaching Philosophy

I believe that effective teaching combines three essential components:

- Strong conceptual understanding
- Practical implementation using programming
- Research-oriented thinking

In my classes, I emphasize visualization, live coding sessions, mathematical intuition, and interactive discussions. Students are encouraged to explore beyond the syllabus through projects, research papers, and open-source software.
